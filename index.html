How technology can restore nature at scale

Pachama has spent years harnessing remote sensing to build a robust platform to observe earth, creating better models to measure and monitor the carbon stored in forests over time.

Estimate carbon

Pachama trains machine learning models using satellite imagery, Lidar, radar and field plots to estimate the carbon stored in forests today and into the future.

Observe deforestation

Using satellite data to compare nearby areas, Pachama observes what would have happened without a carbon project.

Monitor forests

To ensure forests stay standing, Pachama uses remote sensing to detect early signs of disturbance like deforestation, logging or fire.
